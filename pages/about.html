About Us

Welcome to our unique and exciting theme park, where the magical world of Hyrule comes alive. Established in 2023, we offer a captivating blend of adventure, thrill, and fantasy that immerses our guests in the beloved universe of The Legend of Zelda. Each ride, attraction, and service is thoughtfully designed to bring iconic elements of Hyrule to life, from the towering peaks of Death Mountain to the mysterious depths of Zora's Domain. We are proud to provide a fun, safe, and inclusive environment that caters to guests of all ages. Our park encompasses the spirit of adventure and exploration that has defined the Zelda series for decades. Whether you're taking on Ganon in our high-thrill roller coasters, embarking on a Triforce treasure hunt, or savoring the delights at the Kakariko Village Food Court, we're dedicated to making your experience unforgettable. Join us for a journey of a lifetime through the mystical landscapes of Hyrule, right here at our theme park.

FAQ

What are the operating hours of the park?

The park is open from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM from Monday to Sunday.

Are pets allowed in the park?

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in the park. However, we do have a petting zoo at Lon Lon Ranch where guests can interact with our friendly farm animals.

What should I do if I or my child gets injured?

Our Fairy First Aid station is staffed with trained professionals who can assist with minor injuries or health concerns. In case of a major emergency, please dial 911 immediately.

Can I bring my own food and drinks into the park?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed, but we have a wide variety of food options available at the Kakariko Village Food Court.

How intense are the roller coasters?

Our park offers roller coasters ranging from family-friendly to high-thrill. The intensity level for each ride is clearly marked at the entrance.

What attractions are suitable for younger children?

Our Kokiri Forest Adventure and Lon Lon Ranch Petting Zoo are perfect for younger children. We also have the Triforce Treasure Hunt, which is an interactive, family-friendly attraction.

What do I do if I lose something in the park?

Please visit our Lost and Found located near the entrance. You can also inform any of our park employees and they will assist you.

Do you offer any special rates for groups or schools?

We offer discounted rates for groups of 15 or more and for school field trips. Please contact our customer service for more details.

Is the park wheelchair accessible?

Yes, our park is designed to be wheelchair accessible. We also have wheelchairs available for rent at the Guest Services office.

What kind of food options are available at the Kakariko Village Food Court?

The Kakariko Village Food Court offers a variety of options, including burgers, pizza, salads, vegetarian dishes, desserts, and more. We also offer meals designed for children.

Do I need any special equipment or clothing for the "Goron Mountain Climbing" attraction?

No special equipment is necessary. The park provides safety equipment for the attraction. We recommend wearing comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes.

Can I host a party or event at the park?

Absolutely! We offer various packages for birthday parties, corporate events, and more. Please contact our event services team for more information.

Are there lockers available for storing personal belongings?

Yes, lockers are available for rent near the park entrance.

What happens if it rains?

Many of our attractions are indoors or covered, so they remain open in light rain. In case of severe weather conditions, certain attractions may temporarily close for the safety of our guests. Please check our website or social media channels for real-time updates.
